Metric Notes

NOI (Net Operating Income): revenue minus operating expenses (before financing & taxes).
Cap Rate: NOI ÷ total cost or market value; unlevered return snapshot.
Cash-on-Cash: pre-tax cash flow ÷ equity invested; annual yield on actual cash in.
DSCR: Debt Service Coverage Ratio = NOI ÷ annual loan payments; ≥ 1.25× is commonly “bankable.”
IRR: Internal Rate of Return; annualized return including income and sale proceeds over the hold.
Total ROI (Flip): (Net Sale Proceeds – Equity) ÷ Equity; unannualized profit % at sale.
